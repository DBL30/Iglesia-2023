About Us

Our Baptist church, located in southern Costa Rica, is a faith community dedicated to serving people of all ages. We focus on providing a welcoming and safe environment for our youth, and work tirelessly to steer them away from drugs and provide a positive alternative in their lives through our youth worship services and teachings of the Word of God. With more than 10 youth and 20 adults in attendance every Sunday, we share the Word of God with passion and dedication. We strive to be a beacon of hope and a spiritual home for all those seeking a deeper relationship with the Lord.
